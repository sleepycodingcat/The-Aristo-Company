Hey there, I'm Rachell! Ever since I made a silly little short film involving dinosaurs, ice cream, and spontaneous dancing, I've fallen heads over heels in love with filmmaking. Like all student filmmakers, I've worn every hat in production, from directing to editing, or god forbid, attempting to act, but my main interests are cinematography and editing.

There's a quote I love about being a creative because it's so true it stings:

In every creative pursuit, if your taste continually outpaces your skill, you know you're on the right path.

I've always held myself and my work up to frankly, ridiculous standards, but the great thing about that is that I never finish a project without learning in leaps and bounds, or sometimes, through tears and sweat. Even if I can't help cringing while watching my work (I'm sure you know that feeling...), there's nothing to regret because I know I gave it my all.
